Did you know?

Websites use your mouse movements and click activities to identify whether you are a human user [7].

By analyzing user mouse movement data, websites can infer your eye movement and even psychological activities. Where your mouse stays indicates your interest even if you did not click on the item. [8]

You can monitor what information leaves your browser by right-click --> Inspect (or Inspect Elements) --> Network.
